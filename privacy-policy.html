Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 8, 2024

1. Introduction

PolicyGrid ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered insurance analytics platform.

By using PolicyGrid, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, company name, job title, phone number
  • Insurance Data: Policy information, premium data, claims data, loss ratios, and other portfolio analytics data you upload
  • Payment Information: Billing details (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
  • Communications: Messages, demo requests, support tickets, and feedback

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent, search queries
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Cookies and Tracking: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining our insurance analytics service
  • Processing your insurance data and generating insights using AI
  • Personalizing your experience and improving our platform
  • Communicating with you about your account, updates, and support
  • Processing payments and preventing fraud
  • Complying with legal obligations and regulatory requirements
  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve our services

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Data Location

Your data is stored on secure servers located in Bahrain (AWS Middle East Region - me-south-1). We comply with the Kingdom of Bahrain's Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) and relevant GCC data protection regulations.

4.2 Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for data in transit (TLS 1.3)
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • Multi-factor authentication options
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Role-based access controls
  • Secure backup and disaster recovery procedures

Despite our best efforts, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We will never sell your personal information or insurance data to third parties.

5.2 Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting (AWS), payment processing (Stripe), AI services (Anthropic Claude API)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or regulatory authorities
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share specific information

5.3 AI Processing

We use Anthropic's Claude API to process your queries and provide AI-powered insights. Your data is sent to Anthropic's servers for processing but is not used to train their models. See Anthropic's privacy policy for more details.

6. Your Rights

Under Bahrain's PDPL and applicable laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at policygrid.systems@gmail.com

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as:

  • Your account is active
  • Needed to provide you services
  • Required by law or regulatory obligations
  • Necessary for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention)

Upon account deletion, we will delete or anonymize your data within 90 days, except where retention is required by law.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze site usage and improve performance
  • Provide personalized content

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may limit some functionality.

9. Children's Privacy

PolicyGrid is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover we have collected data from a child, we will delete it immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

While we primarily store data in Bahrain, some service providers may process data in other countries. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers, including:

  • Standard contractual clauses
  • Data processing agreements
  • Compliance with applicable data protection laws

11. Changes to This Policy

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending you an email notification (for significant changes)

Your continued use of PolicyGrid after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
